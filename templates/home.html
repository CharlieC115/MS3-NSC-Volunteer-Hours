About This Site

This site is where instructors can record there voluntered lessons and claim expenses.

The current expense per voluntered hour as an instructor/coach is £4.80 with a minimum of £7.20 per lesson. The mileage expense is caluclated by the amount of miles traveled from your house to Norfolk Snowsports club x £0.25

Thank you all for your volunteer time and if you have any questions please contact the office team using the form below.
