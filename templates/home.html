About This Site

Welcome to Norfolk Snowsports Staff Portal. This site is where instructors can record their voluntered lessons and claim expenses. The current expense per voluntered hour as an instructor/coach is £4.80 with a minimum of £7.20 per lesson. For example if you are to do a one hour lesson you will recieve and expense of £7.20 whereas if you were to do a two hour lesson you will recieve £9.60. The mileage expense is caluclated by the amount of miles traveled from your house to Norfolk Snowsports club x £0.25. We greatly appriciate all of our volunteers and they time you give up to teach and coach at Norfolk Snowsports Club. If you have any question or need any help with this site please use the contact form below and a member of staff will get back to you shortly.
